High Level Architecture

Designing the Architecture

Upon joining iCardio.ai it was evident that the company needed a serious architecture overhall. What the firm did have was a lot of data stored on a private server. The two data scientists would connect remotely to the server and build python scripts to extract, parse, and prep the data for model training. Next, they launched jupyter notebooks to conduct the training and display the results. This process was okay for developing proof-of-concept technology that they could then share with the cardiologists on the team for feedback.
